Ranked rules

  • 1v1 — head-to-head race against one opponent, fastest clear wins.
  • 6-player — free-for-all on a shared no-guess board, your finish order determines points and Elo.
  • Tournament — 16 players, the bottom four are cut each early round, then the bottom two, until a 1v1 final.
  • Death penalty — clicking a mine freezes you for 5 seconds before you can play again.
  • Sprint timer — once one player clears, everyone else has 10 seconds to finish.
