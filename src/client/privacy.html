Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026

MSBattle ("we", "us", or "the service") is a free online multiplayer Minesweeper game available at msbattle.net. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have. We collect as little as possible and we never sell your data.

Information we collect

  • Account information from your sign-in provider. When you sign in with Google or GitHub, we receive and store your provider account identifier, your display name, your email address, and your profile picture URL. We use OpenID Connect / OAuth and never see or store your password.
  • Gameplay data. We store data your play generates: your chosen display name, your ranked rating (Elo) and tier, match results, and practice/puzzle attempts. This is used to run the ladder and leaderboard.
  • A session token. After you sign in, a random session token is stored in your browser's local storage so you stay logged in between visits. It is not an advertising or tracking identifier.

We do not run third-party advertising, and we do not use third-party analytics or tracking cookies.

How we use your information

  • To create and maintain your account and keep you signed in.
  • To display your name and avatar to other players in rooms, matches, and on the leaderboard.
  • To calculate and store your ranked rating and match history.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the service.

How your information is shared

Your display name, avatar, and ranked standing are visible to other players as a normal part of multiplayer play and the public leaderboard. Beyond that, we share data only with the infrastructure providers that make the service work:

  • Google and GitHub — identity providers for sign-in.
  • Fly.io — our hosting provider, where the application and database run.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to anyone.

Data retention

We keep your account and gameplay data for as long as your account exists. If you ask us to delete your account, we remove your personal data (account identifier, name, email, avatar, and associated ratings and attempts).

Your rights and choices

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at the address below. You can also revoke MSBattle's access from your Google account permissions page at any time.

Children

MSBattle is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
